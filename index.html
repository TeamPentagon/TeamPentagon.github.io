UNDERGRADUATE RESEARCH TEAM

Team Pentagon was an undergraduate student team founded in 2022 with the goal of advancing research in cutting-edge fields and building high-quality projects with positive societal impact.

Each semester, we challenged ourselves by exploring diverse technical areas such as artificial intelligence, deep learning, blockchain, and privacy-preserving decentralized systems. Working within academic constraints, we delivered robust technical solutions driven by curiosity and a strong desire to learn.
